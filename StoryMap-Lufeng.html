Philadelphia TOD Analysis

Transit-oriented development, or TOD, is a type of community development that integrates a plenty of facilities into a walkable neighborhood and located within a certain buffer of public transportations. Planners believe TOD improves accessibility for people of all ages and incomes.

The study first visualizes the social-demographic information near Philadelphia SEPTA BSL and MFL stations, then analyzes how the distance to transit might influence people's willingess to pay in terms of the property price.

This application has five sections:

1. BSL and MFL at a Glance

2. Median Household Income around SEPTA BSL and MFL

3. Percentage of White around SEPTA BSL and MFL

4. Property price across the entire Philadelphia

5. Property price around SEPTA BSL and MFL
